Are pre-orders required?

Pre-orders are recommended, but not required! We have items available for same-day purchase until sold out. You can also place an order online for same-day pickup.

When do you sell out?

We are a small batch bakery and continuously bake throughout the day. For the day’s updated inventory, look on our online ordering. We encourage pre-ordering if there’s something you really want!

Do you offer custom cakes?

Unfortunately we do not offer custom cakes at this time.

Do you take phone orders?

The easiest way to order is online; however, we do take phone orders. Please be advised that Saturday and Sunday mornings are our busiest times so the line may be busy.

How do I pick up my pre-order?

Please find a staff member and tell them your pickup name.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes! When placing an order, select the delivery option and it will be fulfilled by Doordash. Please note that Doordash sets the delivery range, not us.

Do you offer whole items?

Yes! We offer large format versions of our princess cake, carrot cake, and quiche. These orders can be placed up to 30 days in advance, and must be placed by 8am the day prior.

Do you have anything gluten free or vegan?

While our country loaf and baguette are technically vegan, we unfortunately do not offer any vegan or gluten free items.
